### 5. 🔔 **SISTEM NOTIFICĂRI AUTOMATE**

**Problemă identificată:**
- Notificările NU se creează automat la evenimente
- Clopotelul avea real-time updates DAR fără notificări în DB
- Utilizatorii primeau doar email-uri, nimic în dashboard

**Soluție implementată:**

#### A. Notificări pentru Offers (`src/lib/actions/offers.ts`)

**1. `createOffer` - Notificare Client**
- **Când:** Transporter trimite ofertă nouă
- **Către:** Client (owner-ul shipment-ului)
- **Mesaj:** "You received a new offer of {price} {currency} for your shipment"
- **Link:** `/dashboard/client/shipments/{shipmentId}`

**2. `acceptOffer` - Notificare Transporter**
- **Când:** Client acceptă oferta
- **Către:** Transporter (cel care a trimis oferta)
- **Mesaj:** "Your offer has been accepted by the client"
- **Link:** `/dashboard/transporter/jobs`

**3. `rejectOffer` - Notificare Transporter**
- **Când:** Client respinge oferta
- **Către:** Transporter (cel care a trimis oferta)
- **Mesaj:** "Your offer was rejected by the client"
- **Link:** `/dashboard/transporter/offers`
- **Bonus:** Adăugat query pentru obținere `transporter_id`

#### B. Notificări pentru Shipments (`src/lib/actions/shipments.ts`)

**`updateShipmentStatus` - Notificare Client**
- **Când:** Status shipment se schimbă
- **Către:** Client (owner-ul shipment-ului)
- **Mesaje personalizate:**
  - `picked_up` → "Your shipment has been picked up"
  - `in_transit` → "Your shipment is in transit"
  - `delivered` → "Your shipment has been delivered"
  - `completed` → "Your shipment is completed"
  - `cancelled` → "Your shipment has been cancelled"
- **Link:** `/dashboard/client/shipments/{shipmentId}`

**Fișiere modificate:**
- `src/lib/actions/offers.ts` - import + 3 funcții modificate
- `src/lib/actions/shipments.ts` - import + 1 funcție modificată

**Commit:** `1ad9b86` - "Add automatic notifications for offers and shipment status updates"
